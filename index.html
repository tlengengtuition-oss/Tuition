About Me

A passion for teaching, a track record of results.

Hi, I'm T-Leng — a kind, patient and dedicated private tutor based in Singapore with over 5 years of experience helping students across all levels reach their academic potential. I graduated from Singapore Management University with a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems, and have since worked closely with families across Singapore.

I believe in motivating students through engaging lessons and building their self-confidence. My approach focuses on personalised guidance — helping students not only understand the subject matter, but also develop independent learning skills that will serve them well beyond the classroom.

Whether you need intensive exam preparation, consistent weekly support, or help building a stronger foundation, I'm here to help every step of the way.
